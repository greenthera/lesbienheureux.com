ABSOLUTE BLENDING FREEDOM

THOREAU is a unique spirit made from Guatemalan rums and V.S.O.P. Cognac. It was created as a tribute to Henry David Thoreau, a 19th-century American philosopher, poet, and naturalist: the father of civil disobedience.

THOREAU's priority goes to taste over appellation. The result confirms the adage of all great blends: 1+1=3.

"With 70% rum and 30% Cognac, the exotic banana and 'crème brûlée' aromas of rum combine with the bitter orange notes of Cognac. It is the elegance of Catherine Deneuve with the ardour of Penélope Cruz."

— Alexandre Sirech, CEO

The neck labels feature one of fourteen quotes by Henry David Thoreau. On this bottle:

"The only obligation I have a right to assume is to do at any time what I think right." — H.D. Thoreau.

Tasting Notes

Nose: Crème brûlée, cinnamon, and orange blossom.

Palate: Incredibly smooth and balanced, richness of walnut oil, silky and melting texture.

Finish: Persistent with crystallized fruit and hazelnut.
