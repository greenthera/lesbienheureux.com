Lefort Augustin

Lefort Augustin is a 100% French blended whisky produced by Les Bienheureux at the Distillerie Bercloux in Charente. Launched in 2021, this unpeated 42% ABV spirit is aged in Cognac casks, offering a smooth, fruity, and pastry-like flavor profile designed for quality, accessible enjoyment.

Distilled and aged in France, using local cereals, with a focus on artisanal methods. Known for notes of honeycomb, caramel, and orange zest, with a smooth, round mouthfeel.

Lefort Augustin has received recognition, including accolades from the IWSC, and is widely available with a high value-for-money proposition.

Tasting Notes
Nose: Rich and inviting with aromas of fruitcake, gingerbread, and cereals.
Palate: Smooth, rich and vanilla-like with notes of cereals and dried fruit.
Finish: Powerful with notes of pastry and dried fruit, slightly spicy.
