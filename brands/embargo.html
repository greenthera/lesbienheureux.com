Rum Blend · 40%

EMBARGO LIBRE!

EMBARGO is one-of-a-kind rum. It is the result of three types of rums never blended before: molasses rum (Trinidad & Tobago), cane rum (Guatemala), and agricultural rum (Martinique).

In the Caribbean, there are French-speaking supporters of rhum agricole and English/Spanish-speaking fans of molasses rum. Embargo wanted to synthesize the qualities of these two families of rum — benefiting from the aromatic nose of great agricultural rums and the creamy and digestible palate of molasses rums.

Esplendido — Master Tasting Notes
Color: Old gold with amber highlights.
Nose: Fresh and powerful. Fresh cane, candied lemon, tropical fruit, spices of liquorice and salted butter caramel.
Palate: Sweet spices, silky texture, well-balanced and elegant.
Finish: Long — crème brûlée and custard notes.
